Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Resources

Reading and learning about racist and sexist issues is vital for being informed. However, simply reading about DEI issues is not enough; significant, bold, concrete actions are required to improve academia. Below, we list a number of reading resources, and we commit to using the knowledge we gain to fight for justice and equity in astronomy and academia.
